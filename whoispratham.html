A long time ago, in a galaxy not so far away...

There lived a curious soul named Pratham.

As of today, he's been around for approximately 519,837,600 seconds — that’s over 16 years of figuring life out one line of code at a time.

Born and brought up in Delhi, Pratham is someone who mixes creativity with curiosity. Whether it’s messing around with HTML or diving deep into random late-night thoughts, he’s always building something — even if it’s just for fun.

This website? It wasn’t really planned. Just one of those “I’m bored, let’s make something cool” kind of moments. Turns out, it became a space to tell his story.

He’s into design, storytelling, tech experiments, and lowkey has a soft spot for aesthetics that hit just right.

Music plays a big part in his world — especially Desi hip hop. Artists like Colle$ttye, 3BHK, Seedhe Maut, and even global icons like Drake and The Weeknd are always on repeat in the background while he works on things that may or may not make sense.

This isn’t a portfolio. It’s not a blog either. Just a digital corner where he decided to be... Pratham.

Nothing more. Nothing less.

And yet... people still ask — who is Pratham?
